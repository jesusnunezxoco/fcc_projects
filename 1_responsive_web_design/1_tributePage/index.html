The Life of Juventino Rosas

About

José Juventino Policarpo Rosas Cadenas (25 January 1868 – 9 July 1894) was a Mexican composer and violinist.

History

  • 1868 - Rosas was born in Santa Cruz, Guanajuato, which was later renamed Santa Cruz de Galeana, Guanajuato, and still later into Santa Cruz de Juventino Rosas.
  • 1891 - He worked in Michoacan, reportedly as a member of a millitary band
  • 1892 - Joined an orchest and went on tour in the USA and perofrmed at the World Columbian Exposition World's Fair in Chicago, Illinois
  • 1894 - went for a several-month tour to Cuba with an Italian-Mexican ensemble, where he came down with major health problems. He died there at the age of 26.
  • 1950 - The 1950 film Over the Waves was based on his life
