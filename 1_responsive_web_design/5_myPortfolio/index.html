ABOUT

I'm Jesus but I go by hey-soos. I’m from Austin TX. I graduated from Fairfield University with a B.A. in Communications in 2014. I currently work as an Education Coordinator teaching ESL, workers rights, and occupational health at Worker Defense Project.

My goal is to become an Epidemiologist and to use coding to analyze health data and communicate it in a meaningful way to the public . I have particular interests and experience working with Latino, LGBTQ, and low income communities.
